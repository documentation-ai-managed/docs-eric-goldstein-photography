## Preparation Before Your Shoot

Prepare your shoot effectively to ensure you capture the best moments. Review these common questions to get ready.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What should I prepare before the shoot?" default-open="true">
    Follow these steps to make your session smooth and successful.

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Choose Your Location" icon="map-pin">
        Select outdoor venues in South Florida like Miami Beach or Boca Raton parks. Confirm permits if needed.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Coordinate Outfits" icon=" hanger">
        Pick complementary clothing that matches the event theme. Avoid busy patterns.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Check Weather" icon="cloud-sun">
        Monitor forecasts for outdoor shoots. Have indoor backups ready.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Arrive Early" icon="clock">
        Plan to arrive `15-30 minutes` early for setup and golden hour lighting.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Contact us `48 hours` before for any last-minute changes.
    </Callout>
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How many people can I include in group shots?">
    We accommodate up to `20 people` in group photos. For larger weddings, schedule multiple setups.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

## Cancellation and Rescheduling Policies

Our policies protect both you and our schedule.

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What is your cancellation policy?" default-open="true">
    Cancellations `30+ days` before receive a full refund minus `5%` processing fee. Within `30 days`, `50%` retainer is non-refundable.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="How do I reschedule?">
    Rescheduling is free if `60+ days` notice. Within `60 days`, a `25%` fee applies.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What about weather cancellations?">
    For outdoor shoots, we reschedule free of charge due to rain or severe weather.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>
