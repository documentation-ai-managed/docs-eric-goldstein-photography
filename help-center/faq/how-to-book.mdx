## Overview

Booking is simple and can be done online or by email.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://ericgoldsteinphoto.com/book`
2. Choose your preferred date and location (Miami, Fort Lauderdale, Boca Raton)
3. Fill out your contact info
4. Submit and wait for confirmation email

Or, email info@ericgoldsteinphoto.com with your details.

<Callout kind="info">
A 20% deposit is needed to secure your date.
</Callout>
