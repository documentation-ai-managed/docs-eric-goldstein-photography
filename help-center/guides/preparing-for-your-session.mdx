## Overview

Get the best photos by preparing in advance.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose outfits">
    Wear comfortable, coordinated clothing. Avoid busy patterns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a location">
    Miami Beach, parks in Fort Lauderdale, or central Boca Raton are popular choices.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Arrive early">
    Plan to be at the location 15 minutes before your scheduled time.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Relax and have fun!">
    Eric will guide your poses and capture candid moments.
  </Step>
</Steps>
