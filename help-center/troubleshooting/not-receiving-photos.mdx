---

## Symptoms

- No photo gallery link after 7 days
- Haven’t received an expected email

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your spam folder">
    Galleries are sent via email from `info@ericgoldsteinphoto.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify your email address">
    Ensure you provided the correct address at booking.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Contact support">
    If still missing, email us with your name and session date.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout kind="tip">
Turn on email notifications so you never miss a delivery!
</Callout>
