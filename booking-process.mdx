## Overview

Booking your photography session with Eric Goldstein Photography is straightforward and tailored to capture your special wedding or event moments in South Florida. Whether you're planning a wedding in Miami, an event in Fort Lauderdale, or a celebration in Boca Raton, follow these steps to secure your date. The process typically takes 1-2 weeks from inquiry to confirmation.

<Callout kind="info">
Before starting, ensure you have your event date, location details, and guest count ready. This helps us provide accurate package recommendations.
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

Review these essentials to prepare for a smooth booking:

- Confirm your event date and primary location (e.g., Miami Beach venue).
- Gather key details: expected number of guests, ceremony/reception times, and any special requests like drone shots.
- Decide on your budget range for photography coverage.

## Step-by-Step Booking Process

Use this sequential guide to book your session. Each step builds on the previous one.
