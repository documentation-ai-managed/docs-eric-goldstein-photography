<Update label="2024-10-15" description="v3.0.0" tags={["feature", "improvement"]}>

## New Packages

- Introduced the `Elite Wedding Package` with 12 hours of coverage, two photographers, and a private online gallery for 500+ edited high-resolution images.

- Added `Corporate Event Plus` package featuring live event slideshows and custom album designs.

## Service Area Expansions

- Now serving Palm Beach County, including West Palm Beach and Jupiter, alongside existing coverage in Miami, Fort Lauderdale, and Boca Raton.

## Improvements

- Enhanced booking portal with real-time availability checker.

</Update>

<Update label="2024-09-05" description="v2.1.0" tags={["improvement", "bugfix"]}>

## Pricing Adjustments

- Adjusted base rates by 5% to reflect rising operational costs while maintaining value—`Wedding Essentials` now starts at `$4,500`.

- Introduced flexible add-ons like drone photography (`$750`) and same-day edits (`$300/hour`).

## Portfolio and Gear Updates

- Updated portfolio with 50+ new galleries from 2024 Miami Art Basel events and Boca Raton weddings.

- Upgraded to Sony A1 cameras and DJI Mavic 3 drones for superior low-light performance and 8K video.

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed scheduling conflicts in the client dashboard for multi-day events.

</Update>

<Update label="2024-07-20" description="v2.0.0" tags={["feature", "breaking"]}>
